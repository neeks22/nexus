# Dealership AI Assistant — Onboarding Guide

Welcome! This guide explains what your new AI assistant does, how to manage it, and what to expect.

---

## What the AI Does

Your dealership now has an AI assistant that handles two things automatically:

1. **Instant Responses** — When a new lead comes in through your website, the AI sends a personalized SMS within 60 seconds. It greets the customer by name, mentions the specific vehicle they were looking at, and invites them to connect with your team.

2. **Follow-Up Sequences** — If a lead does not reply, the AI follows up automatically over 30 days (up to 6 messages). Each message takes a different angle — features, value, test drive invitation — so it never sounds repetitive. After 30 days, if there is still no reply, the AI sends a respectful "no pressure" message and moves to monthly check-ins.

When a customer replies with a question about pricing, financing, or trade-ins, the AI immediately hands the conversation to a real person on your team. The AI never discusses prices, monthly payments, interest rates, or financing terms.

---

## What the AI Will Do

- Respond to new leads within 60 seconds via SMS
- Follow up with cold leads on a smart schedule (day 2, 4, 7, 14, 30)
- Detect whether a customer speaks English or French and respond in their language
- Mention specific vehicles from your current inventory
- Hand off to your sales team when the customer asks about pricing or wants to talk to someone
- Respect unsubscribe requests immediately
- Follow all CASL (Canadian Anti-Spam Legislation) rules automatically

## What the AI Will NOT Do

- Discuss pricing, monthly payments, or financing terms
- Make claims about vehicle features that are not in your inventory system
- Contact customers who have unsubscribed
- Replace your sales team — it warms leads and hands them off
- Send messages outside of compliance rules

## Accessing Your Dashboard

Your dashboard is available at the URL provided during setup. Log in with the credentials your account manager gave you.

### Dashboard Views

- **Daily Summary** — See how many leads were handled today, appointments booked, escalations, and average response time
- **Conversation Browser** — Search and read every AI conversation. AI responses are highlighted so you can review what was sent
- **Performance Trends** — Week-over-week charts showing lead volume, reply rate, handoff rate, and appointment rate
- **Settings** — Edit your promotions, inventory highlights, FAQ answers, and blacklisted topics (see below)

---

## Managing Promotions

In the Settings tab, you can add or remove active promotions. The AI will mention these in follow-up messages when relevant.

**How to add a promotion:**
1. Go to Settings in your dashboard
2. Under "Active Promotions," click "Add"
3. Type a short description (max 500 characters), for example: "Spring clearance — 0% financing on all 2024 CR-Vs until April 30"
4. Click Save

**Tips:**
- Keep promotions short and factual
- Do not include specific monthly payment amounts (the AI will not use them)
- Update promotions when they expire — outdated promotions confuse customers

---

## Managing Inventory Highlights

You can flag specific vehicles you want the AI to prioritize in conversations.

1. Go to Settings
2. Under "Inventory Highlights," add vehicles you want to push (e.g., "2024 CR-V EX-L in Platinum White — 14 days on lot")
3. The AI will reference these vehicles when they match a customer's interest

---

## Managing FAQ

Add common questions and answers that the AI can reference:

1. Go to Settings
2. Under "Custom FAQ," click "Add"
3. Enter the question and your approved answer (max 1000 characters)
4. Examples: "Do you offer home delivery?" / "Yes, we offer complimentary delivery within 50 km of the dealership."

---

## Blacklisted Topics

If there are topics you never want the AI to discuss, add them here:

1. Go to Settings
2. Under "Blacklisted Topics," add topics (e.g., "competitor pricing", "staff complaints")
3. The AI will avoid these subjects entirely

---

## Understanding Handoffs

When a customer asks about pricing, financing, trade-ins, or requests a human, the AI creates a handoff. Your team receives:

- A Slack notification with the customer's name, vehicle interest, and conversation summary
- The full conversation transcript
- The AI's recommendation for next steps

Your sales rep should follow up within 15 minutes of a handoff for the best conversion rate.

---

## Language Support

The AI automatically detects whether a customer prefers English or French based on:
- Their CRM profile language setting
- Their phone area code (Quebec area codes trigger French)
- Their postal code

All messages, including follow-ups and handoff templates, are available in both English (en-CA) and French (fr-CA).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I edit what the AI says?**
A: You cannot edit individual messages, but you can control promotions, FAQ, inventory highlights, and blacklisted topics through your dashboard. The base conversation logic is managed by our team.

**Q: What if a customer complains about the AI?**
A: Contact your account manager. We can review the conversation and adjust the AI's behavior if needed.

**Q: How do I add a new sales rep?**
A: Contact your account manager to update your staff list. This ensures handoffs go to the right people.

**Q: What happens if our inventory changes?**
A: The AI references your current inventory data. Keep your inventory CSV or CRM up to date, and the AI will automatically use the latest information.

**Q: Can the AI book appointments?**
A: The AI does not book appointments directly. It warms the lead and hands off to your team, who can then book the appointment. Appointment booking automation is available as an add-on.
