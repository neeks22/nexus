# 30-Day Social Media Content Calendar Template

**For:** Subprime / Near-Prime Car Dealerships in Ottawa
**Market:** Ottawa-Gatineau (bilingual), First Nations communities
**Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok
**Time Investment:** ~2 hours per week per dealer

---

## Weekly Schedule

| Day | Theme | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **Monday** | New Arrival Spotlight | Drive traffic to latest inventory |
| **Tuesday** | Customer Story / Testimonial / Delivery Photo | Build trust and social proof |
| **Wednesday** | Tip / Educational | Position dealer as helpful expert |
| **Thursday** | Inventory Highlight (Carousel) | Showcase multiple vehicles |
| **Friday** | Weekend Sale / Event Promo ("Fresh Start Friday") | Generate weekend foot traffic |
| **Saturday** | Behind-the-Scenes / Fun | Humanize the brand |
| **Sunday** | Coming Soon / Weekly Preview | Build anticipation for next week |

**Best Posting Times for Ottawa Market:**
- Facebook: 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM ET (lunch break) or 7:00 - 9:00 PM ET
- Instagram: 12:00 PM or 6:00 - 8:00 PM ET
- TikTok: 7:00 - 9:00 AM ET or 7:00 - 10:00 PM ET

## Subprime-Specific Content Ideas

### "Your Job Is Your Credit" Campaign
A recurring campaign theme used across posts, ads, and landing pages.

**Core Message:** If you have a job and a driver's license, you're likely approved. We don't judge your past.

**Post Variations:**
1. **The Checklist Post:** "Got a job? Got a license? Got proof of address? You're 90% approved."
2. **The Myth Buster:** "Think you need 700+ credit to buy a car? Wrong. Your employment IS your credit at {{dealership_name}}."
3. **The Comparison:** "Bank said no? We say let's talk. We work with lenders who look at your income, not your history."
4. **The Testimonial:** "{{customer_first_name}} was told they'd never get approved. Today they're 6 months into payments with a rising credit score."
5. **The Simple Ad:** Image of car keys. Text: "Your Job Is Your Credit. Apply Now." CTA button to application page.

### Customer Transformation Stories ("From Denied to Approved")
- Feature real customers (with permission) who were denied elsewhere
- Show the journey: denied > applied with you > approved > driving > credit improving
- Use before/after format for maximum impact
- Collect video testimonials at delivery — even 15 seconds is gold

### Credit Rebuilding Tips Series
Weekly educational content that builds trust and positions the dealer as a helper, not just a seller.

**Topics:**
1. How car payments rebuild credit (reporting to bureaus)
2. The difference between soft and hard credit pulls
3. What happens after a bankruptcy or consumer proposal
4. How long negative items stay on your report
5. 3 things you can do TODAY to start improving your score
6. Why making minimum payments matters more than paying extra
7. How to read your credit report (Equifax/TransUnion)
8. The truth about credit repair companies (spoiler: most are scams)

### "Fresh Start Friday" Weekly Series
- Every Friday features a special deal or vehicle
- Consistent branding: same colors, same format, recognizable at a glance
- Build anticipation: tease it on Thursday night in Stories
- Track engagement over time — this becomes your best-performing recurring series

### First Nations Community Appreciation Posts (ReadyCar-Specific)
- Respectful, genuine content celebrating the communities served
- Highlight tax-exempt purchasing eligibility
- Feature community events, sponsorships, or partnerships
- Use appropriate language (consult with community partners)
- Avoid tokenism — make it about real relationships and real customers
- Seasonal: National Indigenous Peoples Day (June 21), Orange Shirt Day (Sept 30)

### Bilingual Posts for Ottawa-Gatineau Market
- Alternate between English-primary and French-primary posts
- Minimum 2 bilingual posts per week
- Key phrases to always include in French:
  - "Financement disponible" (Financing available)
  - "Votre emploi est votre credit" (Your job is your credit)
  - "On parle francais" (We speak French)
  - "Livraison rapide" (Fast delivery)
- Target Gatineau audience separately in paid ads (French-only)

## Content Creation Checklist

### Weekly Workflow (~2 Hours Per Dealer)

**Step 1: Plan (15 minutes)**
- [ ] Review this calendar for the week's themes
- [ ] Check current inventory — pick vehicles to feature
- [ ] Identify any customer stories or delivery photos from the past week
- [ ] Note any upcoming events, holidays, or promotions

**Step 2: Create Graphics with Predis.ai (30 minutes)**
- [ ] Log into Predis.ai
- [ ] Generate 5-7 posts using the caption templates above
- [ ] Input vehicle details, prices, and dealership info into the {{variables}}
- [ ] Select brand-consistent templates (save favorites for reuse)
- [ ] Download all assets in correct dimensions (1080x1080 for feed, 1080x1920 for Stories/Reels)

**Step 3: Create Ad Variations with Zeely (15 minutes)**
- [ ] Upload top 2-3 inventory photos to Zeely
- [ ] Generate ad copy variations for Facebook/Instagram ads
- [ ] Pick the best 2 variations per vehicle
- [ ] Save for paid campaign use

**Step 4: Edit Video Content with CapCut (30 minutes)**
- [ ] Import any walkaround or testimonial footage
- [ ] Use the video script templates above for structure
- [ ] Add captions (auto-caption feature) — critical for silent scrolling
- [ ] Add dealership logo watermark
- [ ] Export in 9:16 (TikTok/Reels) and 1:1 (Feed) formats
- [ ] Trim to under 60 seconds for maximum reach

**Step 5: Schedule Everything with Buffer or Hootsuite (15 minutes)**
- [ ] Upload all content to the scheduling tool
- [ ] Set posting times per the Ottawa market schedule above
- [ ] Double-check all captions for typos and correct {{variables}} are filled in
- [ ] Ensure hashtags are included (copy from Hashtag Bank)
- [ ] Preview each post before confirming
- [ ] Enable first-comment feature for additional hashtags on Instagram

**Step 6: Quick QA (15 minutes)**
- [ ] Preview the full week in calendar view
- [ ] Verify no two posts look too similar back-to-back
- [ ] Confirm all links work (website, application page)
- [ ] Check that bilingual posts are included (minimum 2/week)
- [ ] Ensure customer photos have signed releases on file
- [ ] Save a screenshot of the scheduled week for client reporting

### Monthly Tasks
- [ ] Review previous month's analytics (reach, engagement, leads)
- [ ] Identify top 3 performing posts and create similar content
- [ ] Update hashtag bank based on performance
- [ ] Collect new customer testimonials and delivery photos
- [ ] Refresh "Your Job Is Your Credit" creative with new visuals
- [ ] Plan any seasonal content (holidays, back-to-school, tax refund season, etc.)
