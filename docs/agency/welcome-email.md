**What happens next:**

**Within 48 hours** — We'll reach out to schedule your kickoff call. Expect a calendar invite from me directly.

**Before kickoff** — Please fill out our onboarding questionnaire. It takes about 15 minutes and gives us everything we need to hit the ground running on day one. [Onboarding questionnaire link]

**At kickoff** — We'll walk through your goals, confirm the technical setup, meet the key people on both sides, and agree on communication rhythms. This is where the build really begins.

**During the build** — You'll get weekly updates (never radio silence), a shared Slack channel for real-time questions, and a mid-point check-in to make sure we're on track.

**At launch** — We'll do a live walkthrough, hand over everything with full documentation, and make sure your team is confident using what we've built.
