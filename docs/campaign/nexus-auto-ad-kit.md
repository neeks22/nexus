## 3. Audience Targeting Recommendations

### Core Audiences

| Audience | Details |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Ottawa CMA + 40km radius (covers Stittsville, Gloucester, Kanata, Barrhaven, Orleans) |
| **Age** | 21-55 |
| **Language** | English and French (Ottawa is bilingual) |
| **Interests** | Used cars, auto financing, car dealerships, Kijiji Autos, AutoTrader, credit repair, debt solutions |
| **Behaviors** | Likely to move, recently moved, online buyers, mobile-heavy users |
| **Exclusions** | Exclude luxury car interests, high-income zip codes (not the target buyer) |

### Custom Audiences to Create

1. **Website Visitors** — Anyone who visited nexusagents.ca/nexus-auto in the last 90 days (install Meta Pixel)
2. **Lead Form Submitters** — Anyone who started but didn't complete the application (retargeting)
3. **Completed Applications** — Exclude from prospecting campaigns to avoid wasted spend
4. **Video Viewers** — Anyone who watched 50%+ of a Reel/Story ad (warm audience for retargeting)
5. **Page Engagers** — Anyone who engaged with the Nexus Auto Facebook/Instagram page in the last 365 days

### Lookalike Audiences

| Lookalike | Source | Size |
|---|---|---|
| **LAL 1%** | Completed applications (best performers) | Start here — highest quality |
| **LAL 1-3%** | All lead form submitters | Scale after LAL 1% is saturated |
| **LAL 3-5%** | Website visitors (90 days) | Broadest reach, use for awareness |

**Note:** Lookalike audiences are limited under Meta's Special Ad Category rules. You cannot use age, gender, or zip code narrowing on top of lookalikes. Meta will auto-expand targeting.

## 4. Campaign Structure Recommendation

### Campaign Objective

**Leads** (Meta Lead Ads or Conversions to landing page)
- Use Meta Instant Forms if you want volume — lower friction, higher quantity
- Use Website Conversions if you want quality — sends to landing page, filters tire-kickers

### Ad Set Structure (Split Testing)

```
Campaign: Nexus Auto — Lead Gen
├── Ad Set 1: Core Interest Targeting (used cars, auto financing)
│   ├── Ad 1 (Income Is Your Credit)
│   ├── Ad 2 (3,500+ Approved)
│   └── Ad 5 (Second Chance)
├── Ad Set 2: Newcomer Targeting (immigration, settlement)
│   ├── Ad 3 (New to Canada)
│   └── Ad 4 (Door Delivery)
├── Ad Set 3: Retargeting — Website Visitors (Custom Audience)
│   ├── Ad 1 (Income Is Your Credit)
│   └── Ad 5 (Second Chance)
└── Ad Set 4: Lookalike 1% — Completed Applications
    ├── Ad 2 (3,500+ Approved)
    └── Ad 4 (Door Delivery)
```

### Budget Recommendation

| Phase | Daily Budget | Duration | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Testing** | $30-50/day across all ad sets | 7-14 days | $210-$700 |
| **Optimization** | $50-100/day, kill losers, scale winners | Ongoing | — |
| **Scaling** | $100-200/day on top performers only | Ongoing | — |

Start at $30/day minimum to give Meta enough data for optimization. Each ad set needs at least $10/day.

### Bidding Strategy

- **Testing phase:** Lowest cost (let Meta optimize)
- **Scaling phase:** Cost cap once you know your target cost per lead (likely $8-25 range for subprime auto)
- **Goal:** Under $20/lead for Ottawa market

## 6. Compliance Notes

### Meta Special Ad Category (MANDATORY)

- **You MUST select "Credit" as a Special Ad Category** when creating these campaigns
- This restricts targeting: no age, gender, or zip code targeting allowed — Meta auto-expands
- Lookalike audiences become "Special Ad Audiences" with broader reach
- Detailed targeting options are limited — some interest categories will be unavailable
- Non-compliance = ad rejection or account ban

### CASL (Canadian Anti-Spam Law) Requirements

- **Express consent required** before sending any follow-up emails or SMS to leads
- Lead forms must include a clear opt-in checkbox (not pre-checked) for marketing communications
- Every follow-up email must include: sender identity, contact info, and unsubscribe mechanism
- Consent records must be stored (who consented, when, how)
- Transactional messages (application status updates) are exempt but cannot contain marketing
- **Implied consent** from a business inquiry lasts 6 months — after that, you need express consent

### What NOT to Say in Ads

| Do NOT Say | Why | Say Instead |
|---|---|---|
| "Guaranteed approval" | Misleading — no lender guarantees approval | "All credit situations welcome" |
| "100% approval rate" | False/misleading claim | "3,500+ applications processed" |
| Specific interest rates (e.g., "4.9% APR") | Rate varies by applicant — misleading if shown as universal | "Flexible financing options" |
| "No credit check" | If a credit check happens at any point, this is false | "Your income is your credit" |
| "Free car" / "Free financing" | Misleading | "Apply at no cost" |
| "Bad credit? No problem!" with guarantee language | Implies guaranteed outcome | "We work with all credit situations" |
| Income claims ("Make $X driving this car") | Unrelated and misleading | Do not use |

### Additional Meta Ad Policy Notes

- Do not use "before and after" style imagery (e.g., sad person vs. happy person with car)
- Do not reference the user's personal attributes directly ("We know you have bad credit")
- Acceptable: "Many Canadians face credit challenges" (general, not personal)
- All landing pages must match the ad's claims — no bait-and-switch
- Privacy policy must be accessible on the landing page
