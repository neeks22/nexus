## NOTES FOR THE SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER

1. **Vehicle Specifics:** The CR-V and other vehicles listed are placeholders. Swap in actual inventory as it arrives. The copy structure and NESB framework remain the same -- just change the vehicle details.

2. **Customer Photos:** Always get a signed photo release before posting customer images. If Sarah M. or any customer isn't available for photos, use the delivery moment with the next real customer who consents.

3. **Moe Confirmation:** Verify with Moe that he's comfortable being featured. Based on Google reviews (Karri CJ Pfeffer, mark cross, and others all mention him by name), he already has strong customer recognition.

4. **Pricing:** All weekly payment figures ($69/week, $85/week, $89/week) are placeholder estimates. Replace with actual approved payment ranges before posting. If unsure, remove specific numbers and use "affordable weekly payments" instead.

5. **Hashtag Strategy:** Keep the core branded hashtags (#ReadyRide, #AllCreditWelcome, #OttawaCars) on every post. Rotate secondary hashtags based on content type.

6. **Engagement Rule:** Reply to every comment within 2 hours during business hours. Every "INFO" or number comment gets a DM within 30 minutes. This is where leads convert.

7. **Meta Compliance:** All posts are written to comply with Meta's Special Ad Category (Credit) requirements. None state or imply knowledge of an individual's credit situation. If boosting any post as a paid ad, run it under the Credit special ad category.

8. **French Versions:** For Gatineau/Quebec reach, translate Tuesday and Friday posts into French. Wednesday educational content also performs well in French for the bilingual Ottawa-Gatineau market.
