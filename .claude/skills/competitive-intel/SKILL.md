# Competitive Intelligence Process

## When to Use
- Before a sales call where the prospect is evaluating alternatives
- When a prospect says "we're also looking at [competitor]"
- Monthly market scan to update positioning

## Key Competitors

| CrewAI | Python | 44K+ | No self-healing. No TypeScript. Crashes when agents fail. |

### Agencies (who we compete with for clients)
| Competitor | Focus | Our Edge |
|------------|-------|----------|
| Generic dev shops | Build anything | We specialize in AI agents. They're generalists. |
| Consulting firms | Strategy only | We build AND operate. They hand you a PowerPoint. |
| AI SaaS tools | One-size-fits-all | We customize. Their tool works until it doesn't. |

## Objection Handling
"Why not just use CrewAI/LangGraph?"
→ "Those are great libraries. But when an agent crashes at 2am, who fixes it? Nexus self-heals. Your pipeline keeps running."

"Why not build it ourselves?"
→ "You could. It'll take 6 months and 2 senior engineers. Or we deploy in 6 weeks with self-healing built in. Which is cheaper?"

"We already have an AI vendor."
→ "What happens when their system goes down? Do they have circuit breakers? Health scoring? Automatic recovery? That's what separates production AI from demo AI."

## How to Use This
1. Dispatch the competitor-analyst agent with the competitor name
2. Review the brief before the call
3. Use the objection handling scripts above
4. Never trash competitors — position on our strengths
