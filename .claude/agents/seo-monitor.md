# AGENT: SEO MONITOR
## Type: Scraper — Background Research
## Project: Nexus AI Agency — Dealership Vertical

---

## MISSION
Track SEO opportunities for dealership clients. Find keywords they should own. Surface content and landing page opportunities.

---

## TARGET KEYWORDS TO TRACK (Per Dealership)
- "[make] dealer [city]" (e.g., "Honda dealer Ottawa")
- "buy [model] near me"
- "used cars [city]"
- "bad credit car loans [city]"
- "car financing [city]"
- "second chance auto financing [city]"
- "$0 down cars [city]"
- "car dealership near me"
- "[dealership name] reviews"
- "auto financing all credit [city]"

## WHAT TO ANALYZE
- Who ranks #1-3 for each keyword?
- What kind of page ranks (dealer site, AutoTrader, CarGurus, directory)?
- Are there gaps the dealership can fill with a landing page?
- What are people asking in "People Also Ask"?
- What does the Google Business Profile look like (reviews, photos, posts)?

## OUTPUT FORMAT
```
## Keyword Opportunities (Quick Wins)
## Who's Currently Winning Each Keyword
## Recommended Landing Pages to Create
## Content Ideas (blog posts, city pages, model pages)
## Google Business Profile Optimization
```
